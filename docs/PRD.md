# Product Requirements Document
## Concept Sentinel
### Overview

Concept Sentinel is an AI-powered tool designed to detect and prevent plagiarism in concept art generated by AI models. The primary goal of this product is to protect the originality and credibility of human concept artists' work, ensuring that their creative efforts are not compromised by AI-generated duplicates.

### Problem Statement

The increasing capabilities of AI models in generating high-quality concept art have raised concerns about plagiarism and the potential devaluation of human artists' work. Current methods for detecting plagiarism are often manual, time-consuming, and prone to errors, making it challenging for artists, art directors, and industry professionals to verify the authenticity of concept art.

### Target Users

* Human concept artists
* Art directors
* Industry professionals (e.g., game developers, filmmakers, architects)
* AI model developers and users

### Goals

1. **Detect AI-generated concept art**: Develop an AI-powered engine that can accurately identify concept art generated by AI models.
2. **Prevent plagiarism**: Provide a tool that can verify the originality of concept art and prevent AI-generated duplicates from being passed off as human-created work.
3. **Protect human artists' work**: Ensure that human concept artists receive credit for their original work and are not unfairly competed with by AI-generated art.

### Key Features (Prioritized)

1. **AI-powered detection engine**: Develop a machine learning model that can analyze concept art and detect AI-generated patterns and characteristics. (High Priority)
2. **Image upload and analysis**: Allow users to upload concept art images for analysis and provide a report on the likelihood of AI-generated content. (High Priority)
3. **Database of known AI-generated art**: Create a database of known AI-generated concept art to improve the detection engine's accuracy and prevent false positives. (Medium Priority)
4. **User dashboard and reporting**: Provide a user-friendly dashboard for artists and industry professionals to upload, analyze, and track their concept art, including reports on detection results and potential plagiarism. (Medium Priority)
5. **Integration with popular art software**: Integrate Concept Sentinel with popular art software (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Sketchbook Pro) to streamline the detection and verification process. (Low Priority)

### Success Metrics

1. **Detection accuracy**: Measure the accuracy of the AI-powered detection engine in identifying AI-generated concept art.
2. **User adoption**: Track the number of users and uploads to the platform, indicating its effectiveness in protecting human artists' work.
3. **Plagiarism prevention**: Monitor the number of prevented plagiarism cases, demonstrating the tool's impact on promoting originality and credibility in the concept art industry.

### Scope

* Develop the AI-powered detection engine and image upload/analysis feature
* Create a database of known AI-generated concept art
* Design and implement the user dashboard and reporting feature
* Integrate with popular art software (initially, focus on 2-3 key software)

### Out-of-Scope

* Developing a full-fledged art creation software
* Providing a marketplace for buying and selling concept art
* Integrating with non-art-related software or platforms

By focusing on the key features and success metrics outlined in this PRD, Concept Sentinel aims to provide a robust and effective tool for detecting and preventing AI-generated concept art plagiarism, protecting the originality and credibility of human concept artists' work.
