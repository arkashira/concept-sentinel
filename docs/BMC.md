# Business Model Canvas – concept‑sentinel  

| **Building Block** | **Details** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • **AI‑Generated Art Plagiarism Detection** – Fast, accurate identification of concept‑art pieces that are derived from or heavily influenced by AI‑generated imagery.<br>• **Credibility Assurance** – Provides artists, studios, and marketplaces with verifiable originality reports to protect brand reputation.<br>• **Automated Prevention** – Real‑time alerts and workflow integrations that stop infringing assets before they enter production pipelines.<br>• **Compliance & Legal Support** – Generates audit‑ready evidence for copyright disputes and licensing negotiations. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Professional Concept Artists & Studios** – Large VFX, game, and animation houses that need to safeguard IP.<br>2. **Freelance Artists & Agencies** – Individuals who sell concept art and need proof of originality for clients.<br>3. **Digital Marketplaces & Asset Libraries** – Platforms (e.g., ArtStation, Unity Asset Store) that host user‑generated art and must enforce content policies.<br>4. **Legal & IP Firms** – Specialists representing creators in infringement cases. |
| **Channels** | • **SaaS Web Portal** – Self‑service dashboard for uploads, scans, and reports.<br>• **API & SDK** – Integration into studio asset‑management systems (e.g., ShotGrid, Ftrack) and marketplace upload pipelines.<br>• **Partner Marketplace** – Bundled offering through design‑tool vendors (e.g., Adobe, Blender) and cloud AI‑service providers.<br>• **Direct Sales & Enterprise Account Teams** – Tailored onboarding for large studios. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription Plans** – Tiered SaaS pricing (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) based on scan volume and report depth.<br>2. **Pay‑Per‑Scan** – On‑demand pricing for occasional users or spikes in workload.<br>3. **Enterprise Licensing** – Annual contracts with custom SLAs, on‑prem deployment, and dedicated support.<br>4. **Professional Services** – Consulting, integration, and forensic audit assistance (hourly or fixed‑price). |
| **Cost Structure** | • **R&D & Model Training** – Compute (GPU clusters), data acquisition, and continuous model fine‑tuning using Axentx’s existing 8.8 M paired datasets.<br>• **Infrastructure** – Cloud storage, CDN for asset uploads, and high‑throughput inference (leveraging vLLM & SGLang).<br>• **Personnel** – ML engineers, backend developers, QA, product management, sales & support staff.<br>• **Compliance & Legal** – Licensing of reference datasets, IP counsel, and audit‑ready documentation tooling.<br>• **Marketing & Partnerships** – Channel partner programs, developer outreach, and industry events. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Proprietary Detection Engine** – Trained on Axentx’s multi‑modal datasets (auto, messages, conversations, query‑resp) to differentiate human‑drawn concepts from AI‑generated outputs.<br>• **Scalable Inference Stack** – vLLM for high‑throughput batch scanning; SGLang for structured report generation.<br>• **Domain Knowledge Base** – Curated catalog of known AI‑art generators, style fingerprints, and artist signatures.<br>• **Customer Success & Support Team** – Ensures adoption and handles dispute evidence preparation.<br>• **Strategic Partnerships** – Cloud providers, design‑tool vendors, and IP law firms. |
| **Key Activities** | 1. **Model Development & Continuous Learning** – Update detection models with new AI‑art generators and emerging styles.<br>2. **Platform Engineering** – Maintain SaaS portal, API, and SDKs; ensure low‑latency scanning.<br>3. **Data Curation & Annotation** – Expand labeled dataset of human vs. AI concept art.<br>4. **Compliance & Reporting** – Generate legally admissible originality reports.<br>5. **Business Development** – Secure marketplace and studio partnerships; run pilot programs.<br>6. **Customer Education** – Workshops, webinars, and documentation on plagiarism risks. |
| **Key Partners** | • **AI‑Art Generator Companies** – For early‑access to model updates (e.g., Stability AI, Midjourney) to improve detection.<br>• **Cloud Infrastructure Providers** – AWS, GCP, Azure for scalable GPU compute and storage.<br>• **Design‑Tool Vendors** – Adobe, Blender, Unity for embedded plugin distribution.<br>• **Legal & IP Firms** – Provide expert validation of reports and co‑market forensic services.<br>• **Industry Associations** – CG Society, GDC, and artist unions for advocacy and standards adoption. |
